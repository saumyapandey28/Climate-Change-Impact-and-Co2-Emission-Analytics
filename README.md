### Interactive Climate Change Impact & CO₂ Emission Analytics Dashboard

Developed an interactive data visualization dashboard to analyze global climate change indicators, CO₂ emissions, air quality trends, economic impacts, and extreme weather events across multiple countries.

**Key Features:**

* Real-time monitoring of climate metrics including temperature, air quality index, CO₂ emissions, and economic impact.
* Comparative country-level analysis of emission trends from 2007–2021.
* Seasonal analysis of economic losses caused by extreme weather events such as floods, droughts, hurricanes, and heatwaves.
* Interactive heatmaps, scatter plots, bullet charts, and trend visualizations for deeper environmental insights.
* Emission change timeline highlighting major climate milestones, including the Paris Climate Agreement (2015).
* Dynamic filtering by country for customized exploration and analysis.

**Tools & Technologies:** Tableau, Data Visualization, Climate Analytics, Interactive Dashboards, Data Storytelling

**Business Impact:** Enables policymakers, researchers, and environmental analysts to identify emission patterns, assess climate-related risks, and support data-driven sustainability initiatives.
